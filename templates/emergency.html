üÜò Emergency Instructions
For Heart Attack:
  • Call emergency immediately
  • Chew and swallow aspirin (if available)
  • Stay calm and sit down
  • Loosen tight clothing
For Stroke (FAST Test):
  • Face: Check if face is drooping
  • Arms: Check if arms can be raised
  • Speech: Check if speech is slurred
  • Time: Call emergency immediately
For Severe Bleeding:
  • Apply direct pressure on wound
  • Elevate the injured area
  • Don't remove embedded objects
  • Call emergency immediately
For Breathing Difficulty:
  • Call emergency immediately
  • Stay in comfortable position
  • Loosen tight clothing
  • Use inhaler if available
